Entry 001 — On Launching a Personal Website in 2026

2026-04-06

*The decision to build a personal website in 2026 is either an act of charming anachronism or quiet defiance. The prevailing wisdom suggests one should instead maintain a "presence" across seven to twelve platforms, each with its own algorithmic feeding schedule.* *A static HTML site, by contrast, simply sits there. It does not optimize for engagement. It does not suggest you might also enjoy other static HTML sites. It is, in the parlance of our times, "not scalable"—which is precisely the point.1*
*"The best technology is the kind that gets out of the way." — Someone, probably, at some point, possibly in a TED talk.*
